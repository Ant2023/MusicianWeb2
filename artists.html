Fatai William

Fatai William is a dynamic Afro Beats and World Music artist hailing from Lagos, Nigeria. With a passion for music that spans genres and cultures, Fatai has quickly risen to prominence with his unique sound, blending traditional African rhythms with contemporary beats. Since moving to Denver, Colorado in 2019, Fatai has continued to push the boundaries of his music, captivating audiences both locally and globally.
